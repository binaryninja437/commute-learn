Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 8, 2025

Welcome to Commute & Learn! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Uploaded Files: PDF documents and images you upload for conversion to audio podcasts.
  • Generated Content: AI-generated scripts and audio files created from your uploaded materials.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including upload frequency and feature usage.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Convert your uploaded notes and documents into Hinglish audio podcasts.
  • Store and manage your generated podcasts in your personal library.
  • Improve our AI algorithms and service quality.
  • Provide technical support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Enhance app performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your uploaded files and generated content are stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For AI-powered script generation and content processing.
  • Google Text-to-Speech: For converting text scripts into audio.
  • Render (Cloud Hosting): For hosting our backend services.

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your uploaded files and generated podcasts for as long as you maintain an active account. You can delete your podcasts at any time through the app. When you delete content, it is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and generated content.
  • Delete your podcasts and uploaded files at any time.
  • Request information about how your data is being used.
  • Opt-out of data collection for analytics purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students of all ages, including minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. International Users

Our services are hosted on servers that may be located in different countries. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside of your residence, which may have different data protection rules.
